    Werther's History And Facts

    Werther's Original was created in 1909 in the town of Werther, Germany by the confectioner Gustav Nebel.

    He used a mix of cream, butter, white sugar, brown rock sugar, and a pinch of salt to create the original Butter Candy.

    Werther's Original now has over a dozen products in over 100 countries!
